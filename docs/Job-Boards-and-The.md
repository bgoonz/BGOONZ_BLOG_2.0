### Update(After The Interview):

As a candidate, there are key next steps that you can and should after every interview to help you stand out.

**Send a thank you email within 24 business hours**

**Do not miss this step!** It takes less than five minutes and can make the difference between you and another candidate. It also keeps you fresh in your interviewers‚Äô memories. For example:

‚Äç

_Hi (name),_

> _Thank you so much for taking the time to meet with me yesterday to discuss my candidacy for (role title). After learning more about (share one or two takeaways from the interview about the company/team‚Äôs priorities), I‚Äôm even more excited to bring my skills in (1‚Äì3 relevant skills) to the team._

> _(your name)_

**Follow up**

Don‚Äôt wait for the company to reach out to you! Be proactive in showing your interest by checking in to see where you stand in the process. If a company indicates a deadline by which you will hear back, and the deadline has passed, follow-up!

**Check your email and phone regularly  
‚Äç\***  
\*Don‚Äôt ghost on a company at any stage in the process; make sure you add their domain to your safe senders list and respond to any messages within 24 hours.

‚Äç

**Be prepared
